Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 20, 2025
Effective Date: June 20, 2025

AutoStreamPro ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our streaming automation platform and services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: Email address, username, password
  • Profile Information: Display name, profile picture, streaming preferences
  • Contact Information: Email address for communications and support

1.2 Platform Connection Data

  • OAuth Tokens: Access and refresh tokens for connected platforms (Twitch, YouTube, Instagram, TikTok)
  • Platform User IDs: Your user identifiers on connected platforms
  • Platform Usernames: Your usernames on connected platforms
  • Content Metadata: Information about your streams, videos, and posts

1.3 Usage Data

  • Service Usage: How you interact with our platform and features
  • Performance Data: Streaming statistics, engagement metrics, view counts
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, log files
  • Analytics Data: User behavior patterns and feature usage statistics

1.4 AI and Content Data

  • Content Analysis: Video/stream content for AI-powered clip generation and optimization
  • AI Settings: Your preferences for automated content creation and posting
  • Generated Content: AI-created clips, thumbnails, and optimized content

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Service Provision

  • Authenticate and manage your account
  • Connect and manage your social media and streaming platforms
  • Generate AI-powered content clips and highlights
  • Automate content posting across connected platforms
  • Provide streaming analytics and insights
  • Optimize content for maximum engagement

2.2 Communication

  • Send service-related notifications and updates
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Send marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Notify you of new features and improvements

2.3 Improvement and Analytics

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our services
  • Develop new features and functionality
  • Ensure platform security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 Platform Integration

We share necessary data with connected platforms (Twitch, YouTube, Instagram, TikTok) to provide our services, including posting content and accessing analytics. This sharing is governed by each platform's terms of service and privacy policies.

3.2 Service Providers

We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our platform, including:

  • Cloud hosting and storage providers (Supabase, Netlify)
  • AI and machine learning services
  • Analytics and monitoring tools
  • Customer support platforms

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government regulation, or to protect our rights, property, or safety.

3.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the transaction.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Access controls and authentication systems
  • Secure OAuth token storage and management
  • Regular backup and disaster recovery procedures

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Specifically:

  • Account Data: Until you delete your account
  • OAuth Tokens: Until you disconnect the platform or they expire
  • Content Data: Until you delete the content or close your account
  • Analytics Data: Up to 3 years for service improvement
  • Log Data: Up to 1 year for security and debugging purposes

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Account Management

  • Access: View and download your personal data
  • Update: Modify your account information and preferences
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Disconnect: Remove connected platforms at any time

6.2 Privacy Controls

  • AI Settings: Control automated content creation and posting
  • Communication Preferences: Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Data Processing: Limit how we use your data for analytics

6.3 Platform-Specific Rights

  • Revoke Access: Remove AutoStreamPro's access to your connected platforms
  • Data Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to certain types of data processing

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and implement appropriate safeguards.

9. Third-Party Links and Services

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites and services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

12. Platform-Specific Privacy Information

12.1 Twitch Integration

When you connect your Twitch account, we access your user profile information and streaming data as permitted by Twitch's API terms. We do not store your Twitch login credentials.

12.2 YouTube Integration

Our YouTube integration accesses your channel information and video metadata to provide analytics and automated posting features. We comply with Google's API Services User Data Policy.

12.3 Instagram Integration

Instagram access is limited to your basic profile information and media content for automated posting and analytics. We follow Facebook's Platform Policy and Data Use Policy.

12.4 TikTok Integration

TikTok integration allows us to access your profile information and video data for content optimization and analytics. We adhere to TikTok's Developer Terms of Service.
