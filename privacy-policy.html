AutoStreamPro ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our streaming automation platform and services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: Email address, username, password
  • Profile Information: Display name, profile picture, streaming preferences
  • Contact Information: Email address for communications and support

1.2 Platform Connection Data

  • OAuth Tokens: Access and refresh tokens for connected platforms (Twitch, YouTube, Instagram, TikTok)
  • Platform User IDs: Your user identifiers on connected platforms
  • Platform Usernames: Your usernames on connected platforms
  • Content Metadata: Information about your streams, videos, and posts

1.3 Usage Data

  • Service Usage: How you interact with our platform and features

1.4 AI and Content Data

  • Content Analysis: Video/stream content for AI-powered clip generation and optimization
  • AI Settings: Your preferences for automated content creation and posting
  • Generated Content: AI-created clips, thumbnails, and optimized content

2. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Cookie Disclosure: AutoStreamPro uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience, analyze usage, and provide our services.

2.1 Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for authentication, security, and basic functionality. These cannot be disabled.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings (e.g., dashboard layout, theme preferences).
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how you use our service to improve features and performance.
  • Third-Party Cookies: Set by integrated platforms (YouTube, Twitch, TikTok) when you connect your accounts.

2.2 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may limit functionality of our Service. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored on your device
  • Delete all or specific cookies
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block all cookies from specific sites

8. How to Delete Your Data and Revoke Access

Important: You have the right to delete your data and revoke AutoStreamPro's access to your connected accounts at any time.

8.1 Delete Your AutoStreamPro Account

To delete your account and all associated data:

  1. Log in to your AutoStreamPro dashboard
  2. Go to Settings ‚Üí Account
  3. Click "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm the deletion

Alternatively, email privacy@autostreampro.com with the subject "Delete My Account" and we will process your request within 30 days.

8.2 Revoke YouTube Access

To revoke AutoStreamPro's access to your YouTube/Google account:

  1. Visit Google Security Settings
  2. Find "AutoStreamPro" in the list of apps with access
  3. Click on AutoStreamPro
  4. Click "Remove Access"

8.3 Revoke Twitch Access

To revoke AutoStreamPro's access to your Twitch account:

  1. Go to Twitch Connections Settings
  2. Find "AutoStreamPro" under Other Connections
  3. Click "Disconnect"

8.4 Revoke TikTok Access

To revoke AutoStreamPro's access to your TikTok account:

  1. Open TikTok app ‚Üí Profile ‚Üí Settings
  2. Go to Security ‚Üí Manage app permissions
  3. Find "AutoStreamPro" and remove access
