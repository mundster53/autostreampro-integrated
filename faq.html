What is AutoStreamPro?
AutoStreamPro is an AI-powered clip automation tool for Kick and Twitch streamers. It monitors your live stream in real time, detects viral moments using audio and visual analysis, generates SEO-optimized titles and tags, and automatically publishes clips to YouTube Shorts, TikTok, and Instagram Reels — with zero manual editing required.
How does AutoStreamPro detect viral moments?
AutoStreamPro uses audio spike detection and visual analysis to identify genuine highlight moments during your live stream — kills, clutches, reactions, and fails. It does not rely on chat activity, which means it works equally well for streamers with zero viewers or thousands of viewers.
Does AutoStreamPro work for small streamers with no viewers?
Yes. AutoStreamPro was specifically built for small streamers. Because it uses audio and visual analysis instead of chat signals, it works whether you have 0 viewers or 1,000. One beta user grew from 26,000 to 350,000+ views in 15 weeks starting with a very small audience.
