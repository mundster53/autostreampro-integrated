Beta FAQ

Straight answers. No marketing fluff.
Will AutoStreamPro make me go viral?
No. AutoStreamPro doesn't control the algorithm. It helps you post more often with less effort so you get more chances to be seen. That's the honest version.
Does it interrupt my gameplay or add overlays?
No. AutoStreamPro watches your stream in the background. No overlays, no pop-ups, no extra buttons to press while you're live.
Which platforms can it post to?
The goal is to handle TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, and native clips where possible — without you manually cutting, exporting, and uploading every single time.
How is this different from the normal free trial?
The free trial lets anyone test the product. The Founders Beta gives you direct influence on how it works, priority support, and a permanent founders discount for helping us shape the final version.
What happens after the beta?
If you love it, you can roll straight into the public release with your founders discount locked in. If it's not for you, you walk away with zero obligation and (hopefully) a lighter content workflow for a month.
